## A Resilient Luxury Market

The luxury real estate sector continues to demonstrate remarkable resilience amid global economic shifts. Prime properties in coastal cities, established suburbs, and emerging luxury destinations are attracting sustained demand from high-net-worth buyers seeking both lifestyle and investment value.

## Key Trends to Watch

### Coastal & Waterfront Premium

Waterfront estates and properties with ocean or lake views command a 15–25% premium over comparable inland homes. Buyers increasingly prioritize outdoor living spaces, private docks, and panoramic views.

### Sustainable Luxury

Eco-conscious design is no longer optional. Solar integration, smart home systems, and sustainable building materials are top priorities for luxury buyers who want elegance without compromising environmental responsibility.

### Investment Migration

Wealthy buyers are diversifying geographically, acquiring properties in multiple markets for portfolio balance. Secondary luxury markets in Portugal, Dubai, and select US metros are seeing significant international interest.
